# Installing integration plug-ins for HCL Connections {#c_plugin_admin_help_over .concept}

Access and update your Connections content from other applications.

Access your HCL Connections data from within Notes or Office apps by adding plug-ins to those other products.

-   **[HCL Connections Desktop Plug-ins for Microsoft Windows](../../connectors/enduser/c_files_window_install_ovr.md)**  
Use the plug-ins to share files and information between Microsoft™ Windows™ applications and HCL Connections or HCL Connections Cloud.
-   **[HCL Connections Add-in for Microsoft® Outlook](../../connectors/admin/c_outlook_connector.md)**  
The power of Connections from your Outlook inbox. Use the Connections Add-in for Microsoft® Outlook to work with Connections content from within your Outlook inbox.
-   **[HCL Connections for Mac](../../connectors/enduser/msdesktop_mac_over.md)**  
The HCL Connections for Mac supports features for you to share and access the latest version of an HCL Connections file.
-   **[HCL Connections Plug-ins for HCL Notes](../../connectors/admin/files_plugin_install_overview.md)**  
Use the HCL Connections Plug-ins for HCL Notes to have easy access to Connections content from the Notes® sidebar.
